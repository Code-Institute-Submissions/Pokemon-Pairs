How to play!

Please follow the instruction below!
Or click here for a guide with pictures to help!

Step 1

First, enter your name! This will appear on the screen shortly afterwards!

Step 2

Then you need to choose a difficulty. Below I have explained the difference between each difficulty level.

Easy

In easy mode, you will have match 6 pairs of cards. They will be random pokémon and random pokémon types.

Medium

In medium mode, you will have to match 6 pairds of cards. However, all the pokémon are the same type. This means the pairs will be the same colour and harder to spot.

Hard

In hard mode, there are only three pokémon. Each pokémon will have two pairs, making it really difficult to tell which one is which.

Step 3

Choose two cards. If they are a match, they will turn blue. If they are wrong, the cards will turn back over. Try to remember where each card is to help you will the game quickly and in the fewest moves.

Step 4

Once you have found all of the pairs, can decide to choose to see which cards were in your game or you can play again, straight away.
